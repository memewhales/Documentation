## Step-by-Step Verification

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

First time on Yoink:

1. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in top right
2. Choose your wallet provider (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
3. Approve connection request
4. Wallet address becomes your account identifier

### 2. Access Verification Page

Navigate to verification:

* Click your **profile icon**
* Select **"Verify Account"**
* Or go to **Settings ‚Üí Verification**

### 3. Link Twitch Account

#### OAuth Connection

1. Click **"Connect Twitch"**
2. Redirects to Twitch login
3. Grant Yoink permissions:
   * Read user information
   * Read stream information
   * Read follower count
4. Redirects back to Yoink
5. Twitch account now linked

#### Verification Checks

System automatically verifies:

* Account age (recommended: 30+ days)
* Follower count (no minimum required)
* Stream history (shows engagement)
* Account standing (no violations)
* Profile completeness

### 4. Social Media Links (Optional but Recommended)

Add additional profiles to strengthen verification:

#### Twitter/X

* Link your Twitter account
* Shows social presence
* Helps prevent impersonation

#### Discord

* Link Discord for community
* Shows community engagement
* Allows direct communication

#### YouTube

* Link YouTube channel
* Additional content platform
* Cross-platform presence

#### Instagram

* Link Instagram profile
* Visual content showcase
* Broader audience reach

### 5. Profile Information

Complete your Yoink profile:

#### Required Fields

* **Display Name**: Your creator name
* **Bio**: Brief description (max 200 characters)
* **Profile Picture**: 512x512px minimum

#### Optional Fields

* **Banner Image**: 1500x500px recommended
* **Location**: Country/region
* **Website**: Personal site or linktree
* **Streaming Schedule**: When you typically stream

### 6. Submit for Review

After completing all steps:

1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Agree to Creator Terms of Service
3. Click **"Submit Verification"**
4. System begins automated review

### 7. Verification Review

#### Automated Checks (Instant - 5 minutes)

System validates:

* Twitch account authenticity
* Social media links
* Profile completeness
* Wallet ownership
* No duplicate accounts

#### Manual Review (if needed)

In some cases, team reviews:

* Flagged accounts
* High-profile creators
* Unusual patterns
* Additional documentation needed

**Timeline**: Usually within 2-4 hours during business hours

### 8. Verification Complete

Once approved:

* ‚úÖ Blue checkmark on profile

## Common Issues

### Issue: Twitch Won't Connect

**Possible Causes**:
* Pop-up blocker active
* Browser extensions interfering
* Already connected to another wallet
* Twitch service issues

**Solutions**:
* Disable pop-up blocker
* Try different browser
* Clear cache and cookies
* Disconnect old wallet first
* Wait and retry if Twitch is down

### Issue: Verification Pending Long Time

**If waiting >24 hours**:

1. Check email for requests for info
2. Verify spam folder
3. Contact support with wallet address
4. Check Discord for platform updates

### Issue: Social Media Won't Link

**Solutions**:
* Ensure accounts are public
* Check account is in good standing
* Try re-authenticating
* Use direct username entry if OAuth fails

### Issue: Verification Rejected

**Common Rejection Reasons**:

* Impersonating another creator
* Fake/bot Twitch account
* Violating platform terms
* Insufficient account history
* Multiple accounts detected

**Next Steps**:
* Review rejection email
* Address specific issues
* Reapply after 30 days
* Contact support if error

## Verification FAQ

### How long does verification take?

**Typical Timeline**:
* Automated: 5-30 minutes
* Manual review: 2-4 hours
* Complex cases: Up to 24 hours

### Is verification permanent?

**Mostly yes**, but can be revoked for:
* Terms violations
* Fraud or scams
* Impersonation
* Extended inactivity

### Can I verify multiple wallets?

**No** - One Twitch account = One verified wallet
* Prevents abuse
* One creator profile per person
* Can change wallet if needed (contact support)

### Do I need minimum followers?

**No minimum** - All legitimate creators welcome
* Focus on authenticity, not size
* Small creators encouraged
* Growth happens on platform

### Can I create tokens without verification?

**No** - Verification required to:
* Create tokens
* Access creator features
* Earn revenue share
* Go live on platform
