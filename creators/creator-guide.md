# Creator Guide

Welcome, creators! Yoink enables you to monetize your content through creator tokens. Your community can speculate on your growth, and you earn rewards from every trade.

## How Yoink Works for Creators

### The Creator Economy Model

On Yoink:

1. **Anyone** can create a token for any creator (even without creator permission)
2. **Traders** speculate on creator tokens through buying and selling
3. **Trading fees** accumulate for the verified creator
4. **Creators** claim their rewards by verifying ownership

{% hint style="success" %}
**Key Benefit**: You earn from trading activity even if you didn't create the token yourself!
{% endhint %}

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Find Your Token

Search for your creator name or Twitch username on Yoink. If a token exists:

* View the token page
* Check trading activity
* See accumulated rewards

### Step 2: Verify Your Account

To claim rewards, you must verify your identity:

1. Connect your wallet
2. Click "Verify Account" or "Claim Creator"
3. Link your Twitch account
4. Complete social verification
5. Verification completes automatically

[Detailed verification guide →](verify-account.md)

### Step 3: Claim Your Rewards

Once verified:

* View accumulated trading fees
* Claim rewards to your wallet
* Track earnings over time

[How to claim rewards →](claim-rewards.md)

## Token Creation

### Creating Your Own Token

You can create a token for yourself or another creator:

1. Click "Create Token"
2. Upload creator image
3. Set token name and symbol
4. Write description
5. Deploy token (requires SOL for transaction)

[Complete token creation guide →](create-token.md)

### Token Information

Include in your token:

* **High-quality image**: Creator logo or profile picture
* **Engaging description**: Your brand, content style, streaming schedule
* **Social links**: Where followers can find you
* **Unique value**: What makes your content special

## Earning Rewards

### Revenue Streams

As a verified creator, you earn from:

| Revenue Source | How It Works |
|----------------|--------------|
| **Trading Fees** | ~40% of all buy/sell transactions |
| **Volume Bonuses** | Increased earnings on high-volume days |
| **Creator Bonuses** | Special platform rewards for top creators |

### Maximizing Earnings

To increase your trading volume and rewards:

1. **Stream Consistently**: Regular streams = regular trading
2. **Engage Community**: Active community = more holders
3. **Promote Token**: Share your token with followers
4. **Quality Content**: Better content = more speculation
5. **Go Viral**: Viral moments = massive volume spikes

{% hint style="info" %}
**Pro Tip**: Announce your streaming schedule! Tokens often see increased activity when creators go live.
{% endhint %}

## Common Questions

### Do I need to create my own token?

No! Community members can create tokens for creators. You just need to verify ownership to claim rewards.

### What if multiple tokens exist for me?

The first verified token becomes the official one. Contact support about duplicates.

### Can I delete my token?

Tokens cannot be deleted due to blockchain permanence. However, you can choose not to verify/claim rewards.

### What about taxes?

Trading fee rewards may be taxable income. Consult a tax professional in your jurisdiction.

### Can I lose my verification?

Yes, violations of terms of service can result in verification removal.
