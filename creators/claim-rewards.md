# Claim Your Rewards

Learn how to claim your earnings as a creator or Early Bird holder on Yoink.

## Creator Rewards

### How Creator Earnings Work

As a token creator, you earn from:

* **Trading Fees**: ~40% of all buy/sell fees
* **Automatic Accumulation**: Collected with each trade
* **All Your Tokens**: Combined from all tokens you created
* **Perpetual**: Earnings continue as long as trading occurs

### View Your Creator Earnings

Access your earnings dashboard:

1. Go to your **Profile**
2. Click **"Creator Dashboard"**
3. View **"Rewards"** section

**Dashboard Shows**:
* **Total Unclaimed**: SOL available to claim
* **Total Claimed**: Historical earnings
* **Per Token Breakdown**: Earnings by each token
* **7-Day Revenue**: Recent earnings trend
* **All-Time Revenue**: Lifetime earnings

### Claiming Creator Rewards

#### Step-by-Step Process

1. Navigate to **Creator Dashboard**
2. Locate **"Claim Rewards"** section
3. Review available amount
4. Click **"Claim All"** button
5. Confirm transaction in wallet
6. SOL deposited to your wallet

#### Batch Claiming

If you have multiple tokens:

* **Claim All**: One transaction for all tokens
* **Claim Individual**: Select specific tokens
* **Gas Efficient**: Batch claiming saves fees

### Claiming Frequency

**Recommended**:
* **Weekly**: Balance gas costs vs convenience
* **Monthly**: Lower gas fee percentage
* **After Major Events**: Post-stream, after pumps
* **Minimum Amount**: Wait until >0.1 SOL to claim

**Gas Costs**:
* ~0.00001-0.0001 SOL per claim
* Negligible compared to rewards
* Batch claiming more efficient

## Early Bird Rewards

### How Early Bird Earnings Work

As an Early Bird holder:

* **Trading Volume Share**: Earn from all trades in your token
* **20-Way Split**: Divided among all Early Birds
* **Never Sell Rule**: Must hold to keep earning
* **Automatic Accumulation**: Collected continuously
* **Multiple Seats**: Can hold multiple across different tokens

### View Your Early Bird Earnings

Check your rewards:

1. Go to your **Profile**
2. Click **"Early Bird Rewards"**
3. View earnings breakdown

**Page Shows**:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Token** | Which token's seat |
| **Seat #** | Your position (1-20) |
| **Unclaimed** | Available to claim |
| **Total Earned** | All-time rewards |
| **7D Volume** | Recent trading activity |
| **Status** | Active/Forfeited |

### Claiming Early Bird Rewards

#### Step-by-Step Process

1. Navigate to **Early Bird Rewards** page
2. Review available rewards by token
3. Click **"Claim"** next to token
   * Or click **"Claim All"** for all tokens
4. Confirm transaction in wallet
5. SOL deposited immediately

#### Multiple Token Seats

If you're Early Bird on multiple tokens:

* **Claim Individually**: One token at a time
* **Claim All**: Single transaction for all
* **Selective Claiming**: Choose which to claim
* **Gas Optimization**: Batch when possible

### Early Bird Reward Frequency

**Best Practices**:

* **High Volume Tokens**: Claim weekly
* **Moderate Volume**: Claim bi-weekly/monthly
* **Low Volume**: Claim when convenient
* **After Big Events**: Post-stream, after viral moments

**Strategic Timing**:
* Claim before gas fee spikes
* Batch with other transactions
* Set calendar reminders
* Don't let rewards accumulate too long

**Strategies**:

1. **Batch Claims**: Claim all rewards at once

4. **Standard Priority**: Don't overpay for speed

## Common Issues

### Issue: Claim Button Disabled

**Possible Reasons**:

* No rewards accumulated yet
* Minimum claim amount not reached
* Network issue
* Wallet not connected

**Solutions**:
* Check "Unclaimed" amount is >0
* Wait for more rewards to accumulate
* Refresh page and reconnect wallet
* Try different browser/clear cache

### Issue: Transaction Failed

**Causes**:
* Insufficient SOL for gas
* Network congestion
* Wallet rejection
* Contract error

**Solutions**:
* Ensure ~0.01 SOL for gas
* Wait and retry during off-peak
* Check wallet settings
* Contact support if persists

### Issue: Missing Rewards

**Check These**:
* Correct wallet connected
* Recent trades occurred
* Not viewing wrong token
* Rewards haven't been claimed already

**Verify**:
* Check transaction history
* View on blockchain explorer
* Confirm wallet address
* Contact support with details

### Issue: Early Bird Seat Forfeited

**If you sold any tokens**:
* Seat is permanently lost
* No way to recover
* Stop accumulating rewards
* Can't claim forfeited seat rewards

**Going Forward**:
* Claim accumulated rewards before forfeiture
* Learn from mistake
* Be more careful with future seats
* Only buy Early Bird if committed to hold
