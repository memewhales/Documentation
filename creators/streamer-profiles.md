# Streamer Profiles

## Overview

Streamer profiles on Yoink showcase content creators who are actively streaming on Twitch and have tokens created for them. These profiles serve as a hub for fans, traders, and community members to engage with their favorite creators.

## What is a Streamer Profile?

A streamer profile is a dedicated page that displays:

* **Creator Information**: Name, bio, and social links
* **Token Details**: Trading information and market data
* **Live Status**: Whether the creator is currently streaming
* **Stream Integration**: Embedded live stream (when active)
* **Trading Interface**: Direct buy/sell functionality
* **Community Activity**: Chat, trades, and holder information
