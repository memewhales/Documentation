## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is YOINK required to use the platform?

**No**. You can trade creator tokens with just SOL. YOINK provides benefits but isn't mandatory.

### How are YOINK buybacks funded?

50% of platform treasury (from trading fees) is used to buy YOINK from the market and burn it.

### Can YOINK be mined?

No traditional mining. Earn through:
* Platform usage rewards
* Staking
* Liquidity provision
* Referrals

### What's the max supply?

1 billion tokens at launch, but decreasing through burns. No inflation - fixed initial supply.

### When governance?

Full governance launching Q2 2025. Initial proposals will be temperature checks before that.

### How to stake YOINK?

Go to platform, connect wallet, navigate to Staking section, choose lock period, stake tokens.
