# How to Sell Tokens

Learn when and how to sell your creator tokens on Yoink while understanding the implications for Early Bird status.

* Are you an Early Bird? (Check your profile)
* Have you achieved your profit target?
* Is this the right time to exit?
* Do you understand tax implications?

## Step-by-Step Selling Guide

### 1. Access Your Token

Navigate to the token you want to sell:

* Go to your **Profile**
* View your **Holdings**
* Click on the token card
* Or search for token directly

### 2. Open Sell Widget

On the token page:

1. Locate the **Buy/Sell widget**
2. Click the **"Sell"** tab
3. Widget shows your token balance

### 3. Enter Amount to Sell

Choose how much to sell:

#### Option A: Enter Token Amount

* Type the number of tokens to sell
* Widget calculates SOL you'll receive

#### Option B: Use Percentage Buttons

* **25%**: Sell quarter of your holdings
* **50%**: Sell half position
* **75%**: Sell three-quarters
* **100%**: Exit entire position (WARNING: Loses Early Bird)

#### Option C: Enter SOL Amount

* Specify SOL amount you want to receive
* Widget calculates tokens needed

### 4. Review Sell Details

The widget displays:

| Detail | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **You Sell** | Token amount being sold |
| **You Receive** | SOL you'll get (estimated) |
| **Price Impact** | How your sale affects price |
| **Slippage Tolerance** | Max acceptable price change |
| **Trading Fee** | Platform fees (from received SOL) |
| **Minimum Received** | Worst-case SOL amount |

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Price Impact**: Selling large amounts can significantly drop the price. Consider selling in batches for better execution.
{% endhint %}

### 5. Adjust Slippage (If Needed)

For sell orders:

* **Standard**: 1-2% slippage
* **Large Orders**: 3-10% slippage
* **Volatile Tokens**: 5-15% slippage
* **Low Liquidity**: May need higher slippage

### 6. Confirm Sale

1. Review all details carefully
2. **Final Warning**: Check Early Bird status
3. Click **"Sell"** button
4. Confirm transaction in wallet
5. Approve the transaction
6. Wait for confirmation

### 7. Transaction Complete

After confirmation:

* ‚úÖ SOL deposited to your wallet
* ‚úÖ Tokens removed from balance
* ‚úÖ Transaction recorded in history
* ‚ö†Ô∏è Early Bird status lost (if applicable)

## Managing Large Positions

### For Big Holdings

If selling large position:

1. **Split the Sale**: Multiple smaller sells
2. **Time It**: Spread over hours/days
3. **Monitor Impact**: Watch price reaction
4. **Adjust Strategy**: Based on market depth

### Example: Selling 1M Tokens

Instead of selling all at once:

* Day 1: Sell 250k tokens
* Day 2: Sell 250k tokens  
* Day 3: Sell 250k tokens
* Day 4: Sell remaining 250k tokens

**Benefits**:
* Less price impact
* Better average exit price
* Maintains market stability
* Allows others to buy dips

### Mistake 1: Emotional Selling

* Selling due to fear or panic

* Not sticking to plan

**Solution**: Have exit strategy before buying

### Mistake 2: Selling Too Early

## Sell Order Errors

### Insufficient Balance

**Error**: "Insufficient token balance"

**Solution**:
* Check you own enough tokens
* Reduce sell amount
* Verify correct token selected

### Slippage Exceeded

**Error**: "Slippage tolerance exceeded"

**Solution**:
* Increase slippage tolerance
* Reduce sell amount
* Wait for more liquidity
* Sell in smaller batches

### Low Liquidity

**Error**: "Insufficient liquidity"

**Solution**:
* Reduce sell amount significantly
* Wait for more buyers
* Increase slippage substantially
* Consider gradual exit
