# What is Graduation

## Overview

Graduation is the process where a token successfully completes its bonding curve phase and transitions to a decentralized exchange (DEX) with permanent liquidity on Raydium.

## How Graduation Works

### The Bonding Curve

When a coin is first created on Yoink, it starts with a bonding curve mechanism:

* **Initial Phase**: The token trades on an internal bonding curve
* **Liquidity Building**: As users trade, liquidity accumulates
* **Progress Tracking**: You can see the graduation progress on each coin's page

### Reaching 100%

The graduation happens when the bonding curve reaches **100% completion**:

1. **Trading Activity**: Users buy and sell the token, gradually filling the curve
2. **Liquidity Accumulation**: Trading fees and volume build up the liquidity pool
3. **Automatic Graduation**: Once 100% is reached, the migration happens automatically

## FAQs

### How long does graduation take?

It varies based on trading activity. Popular tokens can graduate in days, while others may take weeks or longer.

### Can a token fail to graduate?

Tokens don't "fail" - they simply remain on the bonding curve until reaching 100%. There's no time limit.

### What happens to my tokens when it graduates?

Your tokens remain in your wallet. You can continue trading them on Raydium after graduation.

### Do I need to do anything during graduation?

No! The migration is completely automatic. You don't need to take any action.

### Can I still earn Early Bird rewards after graduation?

Yes! Early Bird status is permanent as long as you don't sell your tokens.
