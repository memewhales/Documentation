Graduation is the moment when a creator token "grows up" and moves from Yoink's bonding curve to a traditional decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium. It's a major milestone that benefits everyone involved.

## The Progress Bar

Every token on Yoink has a **progress bar** that shows how close it is to graduation. This progress is based on the token's market cap:

* **0%**: Just launched, very low market cap
* **50%**: Growing community, moderate market cap  
* **90%**: High demand, approaching graduation
* **100%**: Graduated! Now trading on DEX

The progress bar gives you a clear visual of where the token stands in its journey.

## What Triggers Graduation?

## The Graduation Process

When a token hits 100% progress, here's what happens automatically:

### 1. **Liquidity Pool Creation**
* A new trading pool is created on Raydium DEX
* All SOL from the bonding curve becomes permanent liquidity
* The token becomes a standard SPL token

### 2. **Seamless Transition**
* Trading continues immediately on the DEX
* Current holders keep all their tokens
* Price discovery becomes more mature
* Much deeper liquidity available

### 3. **Continued Integration**
* Token still appears on Yoink platform
* Early Bird rewards continue flowing
* Creator fees still collected
* All platform features remain active

## Before vs After Graduation

| Aspect | Pre-Graduation (Bonding Curve) | Post-Graduation (DEX) |
|--------|--------------------------------|----------------------|
| **Liquidity** | Contract-provided | Pool-based |
| **Trading Volume** | Platform users only | Entire Solana ecosystem |

## What Happens to Your Holdings?

**Nothing changes for your tokens!**

* You keep exactly the same amount
* Your Early Bird status continues (if you have it)
* Your tokens work on both Yoink and DEX
* No action required from you

The only difference is where you can trade them - now you have more options.
